Get Involved

RightsStatements.org is run by a consortium consisting of Europeana, the Digital Public Library of America (DPLA), the National Digital Library of India, Library and Archives Canada and Trove at the National Digital Library of Australia. We provide a set of standardized rights statements that answers the needs of cultural heritage institutions and aggregators across the globe. If you are interested in joining the consortium, contributing to the development of the rights statements we offer, or if you have questions, please get in touch with us info@rightsstatements.org.
